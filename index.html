about

You cannot teach a man anything; you can only help him discover it in himself.
- Galileo

Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani is one of the premier engineering colleges in the country. APOGEE is the annual, international technical festival of BITS Pilani and we are proud to announce that we are back with the 37th edition of one of the largest technological symposiums. Apart from a melange of technical events, Apogee also hosts Aarohan, a Pan-India school level examination.

Crafted and conducted by the students of BITS Pilani under the guidance of a dedicated panel of professors, the paper will have one unified theme based on the various extraordinary scientific phenomenons at the boundaries of human scientific achievement, which can be understood and solved using a reasonable amount of knowledge in basic sciences and logical reasoning. The aim is to inculcate the much needed scientific temperament.

Since its inception in 2012, what started off as a humble attempt to rekindle curiosity is now a nationwide event. We also made a mark at the international stage with participation from Abu Dhabi and Germany. This year we are expanding our reach to over 30 cities with over 100 BITS Pilani associates and an estimated participation of over 10,000 students.

Round 1

  • The paper will be Multiple Choice based.
  • The duration of the paper will be two hours.
  • The paper will have one unifying theme based on recent scientific breakthroughs and phenomena.
  • The questions based on that will be entirely application based and not factual.
  • The syllabus comprises of the syllabus of the students’ respective class. The students will have to get innovative with the basic formulae and improvise.
  • The marking scheme will be revealed at the time of examination.
  • The school and city toppers will get a certificate of merit stating the same and would also get exciting prizes according to their ranks.
