about

A wonderful learning experience.

AAROHAN is a national level examination crafted and conducted by the students of BITS Pilani in schools. The examination is conducted for classes IX, X, XI and XII. The aim of this examination is to inculcate scientific temperament and get the coming generation interested towards scientific and technical development studies. All that only at a cost of ₹150 per student.

Apart from a first-hand exposure to a national level exam and an opportunity to compete with students from schools across India, Aarohan has a lot more to offer. You will get an ‘All India Rank’(AIR) and a certicate of participation. The school and city toppers will get a certificate of merit stating the same and would also get exciting prizes according to their ranks. The nal winners will be invited to BITS Pilani’s technical fest APOGEE. They’ll have access to all the events and workshops happening on the campus and we’ll have round two and some other events exclusively for them. And on a materialistic and tangible scale, there’ll be exciting goodies and prizes as well as the opportunity to interact with the most progressive minds of the country.

sample paper challenge

With Aarohan taking a huge a leap forward in the present day education system through an "Out-of-the-Box" paper, we present to you a tinge of scientific curiosity coupled with an incentive you can't refuse. Its not just a test of wits. Its a challenge. So bring out the scientist in you and take the Aarohan Sample Paper Challenge.

The sample paper is based on Space Technology and has 9 questions only, unlike the actual paper. The students can take this challenge online by logging into www.qalaxia.com They can create a free account on this website and go to the Aarohan circle and take the sample paper challenge. Selected students will get to write Aarohan free of cost.
