About

You cannot teach a man anything; you can only help him discover it in himself.
- Galileo

Aarohan is a pan India school level examination for classes 9th to 10th and 11th to 12th ( science and mathematics ) hosted by APOGEE, the annual, international technical festival of BITS Pilani.

Crafted and conducted by the students of BITS Pilani under the guidance of a dedicated panel of professors, the paper will have one unified theme based on the various extraordinary scientific phenomenons at the boundaries of human scientific achievement, which can be understood and solved using a reasonable amount of knowledge in basic sciences and logical reasoning.

The aim is to inculcate the much needed scientific temperament

Round 1

  • The paper will be:-
    • 1. One word answer based
    • 2. Subjective based
    • 3. Integer based
    • 4. Objective based

  • But what will the paper ask?
    Simple; answers to the questions in it ( bad joke! ). But more than that, the paper will ask you to go on a thrilling adventure inside your brain and look out for all the different possibilities of even simple things that you observe in daily life.

  • Well, that sounded heavy, what exactly do I have to study to prepare for the exam?
    The syllabus comprises of the syllabus of the students’ respective class. RELAX! You don’t have to mug up facts or anything. You only need to prepare yourself to learn something new from the paper. The paper will have one unifying theme based on recent scientific breakthroughs and phenomena. The answers will be in the questions itself (your teacher says that too), but just like your teacher, we mean it. You just have to think differently, you don’t require any extra knowledge to get to the answers, and you can find them with what you know already.

  • Ok, but what’s the marking scheme?
    Uh-huh! It’s a secret, shall be disclosed to you only on the day of exam.
